# DALL-E 3用 ロゴプロンプト（簡潔版）
# 無料プロトタイプ作成用 - 方向性確認後、Nanobanana Proで本番生成

Professional minimalist wordmark logo for "StudioJinsei"

**Core Design:**
- Text-only, no symbols or icons
- Font: Modern sans-serif (Poppins-style)
- "Studio" in light weight (300-400), "Jinsei" in bold weight (600-700)
- Plenty of white space (30%+ margins)
- Square 1024x1024px format

**Colors:**
- Text: Dark teal (#1D4E4A)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) or soft mint green (#A8D5BA)
- Keep it simple: 2-3 colors maximum

**Layout:**
- Horizontal layout preferred for versatility
- Balanced composition with generous spacing
- Must be legible at small sizes (256px)

**Style:**
- Ultra-minimalist, Apple-inspired aesthetic
- Clean, modern, trustworthy, approachable
- NO decorative elements, gradients, or shadows
- NO partial underlines (like "sei" only) - avoid fragmented accents

**Key Constraints:**
- Simple is better - less is more
- High quality, attention to detail
- Professional yet personal, sophisticated yet friendly
